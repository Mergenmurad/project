Tour Details

In Search of Adventure

Dashoguz - Darvaza - Ashgabat - Mary - Farap

Embark on an exciting journey through Turkmenistan, exploring ancient historical sites and natural wonders. From the UNESCO World Heritage sites of Kunya-Urgench and Merv to the famous Darvaza gas crater, this tour offers a perfect blend of history, culture, and adventure.

Itinerary

Day 1

  • Meet at the border crossing in Dashoguz.
  • Excursion to the historical sights of Kunya-Urgench (UNESCO World Heritage Site): the mausoleums of Sultan Sanjar, Kutlug-Timur, and Tuglu-Timur Khan, the Gumbezi-Ahmed Madrasah, and the Kyalya Minaret.
  • Visit the famous Darvaza gas crater, followed by a traditional dinner and overnight stay in a Turkmen yurt.

Day 2

  • Transfer to Ashgabat.
  • Visit the Ruhy Turkmenbashi Mosque, the largest mosque in Turkmenistan.
  • Sightseeing tour of Ashgabat, including Independence Monument, Arch of Neutrality, Constitution Monument, Alem Cultural and Entertainment Center, Magtymguly Fragi Park, Memorial Complex, and Wedding Palace.

Day 3

  • Visit the mountain village of Nokhur to explore its ancestral cemetery, waterfall, and old plane tree.
  • Transfer to the natural underground lake Kovata.
  • Arrival in Ashgabat in the evening.

Day 4

  • Drive from Ashgabat to the city of Mary.
  • Sightseeing of Ancient Merv, one of the most ancient regions of Central Asia.
  • Visit the mausoleum of Sultan Sanjar, Small and Big Gyz-Kala, the mausoleum of Ibn Zeyd, and the fortresses of Erk-Kala and Gyaur-Kala.
  • Check-in at the hotel.

Day 5

  • Transfer to the border crossing at Farap for departure.
