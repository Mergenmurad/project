Places to explore

  • Ashgabat City

    Explore the white marble capital of Turkmenistan, Ashgabat, with its unique architecture and vibrant markets.

  • Darvaza Gas Crater

    Witness the mesmerizing 'Door to Hell' at Darvaza, a natural gas crater that has been burning for decades.

  • Yangykala Canyon

    Marvel at Yangykala Canyon's stunning rock formations and vibrant colors.

  • Ancient Merv

    Explore Ancient Merv, the legendary Silk Road city with extensive ruins and rich cultural history.

  • Kunya-Urgench

    Explore Kunya-Urgench and discover the ancient monuments of this historic Silk Road city.

  • Koytendag

    Visit Koytendag to explore stunning landscapes and the Dinosaur Plateau, where ancient footprints are preserved.

  • Parthian Fortresses of Nisa

    Explore Nisa, the ancient capital of the Parthian Empire, where history meets stunning ruins.

  • Underground lake Kov-Ata

    Discover Kov Ata, a stunning underground lake with healing thermal waters.
