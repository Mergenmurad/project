Tour Details

From Paradise to the "Gates of Hell"

Ashgabat - Mary - Darvaza - Ashgabat

Embark on an exciting journey through Turkmenistan, exploring ancient historical sites and natural wonders. From the UNESCO World Heritage sites of Old Nisa and Merv to the famous Darvaza gas crater, this tour offers a perfect blend of history, culture, and adventure.

Itinerary

Day 1

  • Meeting at Ashgabat International Airport.
  • Transfer and check-in at the hotel.
  • Excursion to Old Nisa, included in the UNESCO World Heritage List as one of the first capitals of the Parthian Empire.
  • Visit the Turkmenbashi Ruhi Mosque, the largest mosque in Turkmenistan.

Day 2

  • Trip from Ashgabat to the city of Mary.
  • On the way, visit historical sights including Abiverd and Seyitjemaleddin Mosque.
  • Inspection of ancient Merv: Mausoleum of Sultan Sanjar, Small and Large Gyz-Kala, Mausoleum of Ibn Zeid, Erk-Kala, and Gyaur-Kala.
  • Check-in at the hotel in Mary.

Day 3

  • Trip from Mary to Ashgabat through the Karakum desert to the Darvaza gas crater.
  • Enjoy sightseeing and a walk around the crater, followed by a traditional dinner and overnight stay in a Turkmen yurt.

Day 4

  • Return to Ashgabat.
  • Visit the private horse farm Gokdepe and take a trip to the underground lake Kov-Ata, a natural underground lake.

Day 5

  • Visit to the local bazaar "Altyn Asyr" (Tolkuchka).
  • Sightseeing tour of Ashgabat, including Independence Monument, Arch of Neutrality, Constitution Monument, Alem Cultural and Entertainment Center, Magtymguly Fragi Park, Memorial Complex, and Wedding Palace.

Day 6

  • Excursion to the National Museum of Turkmen Carpets.
  • Visit to the local market "Gulistan" (Russian bazaar).
  • Enjoy a night city tour.

Day 7

  • Visit to the shopping and entertainment centers "Berkarar", "Ashgabat", and "Arkach".
  • Transfer to the airport for departure.
