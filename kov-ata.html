Kov Ata

Kov Ata is an underground lake in an eponymous cave near Baherden in Turkmenistan. This natural wonder is famous for its thermal waters, which are believed to have therapeutic properties. The lake is surrounded by stunning limestone formations, creating a captivating and mystical atmosphere. Visitors can explore the lake and its unique geological features, making it a popular destination for adventurers and nature enthusiasts. The warm waters offer a relaxing experience, and the serene environment adds to its allure as a hidden treasure in the region.

Explore Kov Ata
  • Underground Lake: Swim in the warm, mineral-rich waters of Kov Ata, known for their healing properties.
  • Stunning Surroundings: Enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding caves and limestone formations.
  • Nature Trails: Explore hiking trails around the area that lead to picturesque landscapes and scenic overlooks.
  • Cultural Experience: Learn about the local legends and the significance of Kov Ata in Turkmen culture.
  • Relaxation: Take a break from your travels and rejuvenate in the tranquil atmosphere of this natural site.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Kov Ata is during spring (April to June) and autumn (September to November) when the weather is pleasant for outdoor activities.
