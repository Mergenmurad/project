About Us

Discover Our Journey and Mission

Learn about who we are and what drives us

Welcome to Gatnashyk Travel! We are a young, dynamic company with a team of enthusiastic and passionate young staff. Our journey began with a deep love for showcasing the beauty and uniqueness of Turkmenistan. Our team of travel experts is dedicated to offering memorable and immersive experiences that highlight the rich culture, history, and landscapes of this incredible destination.

At Gatnashyk Travel, we believe that every journey should be an adventure. We work closely with local guides and partners to ensure that each tour is tailored to meet your interests and preferences. Our commitment to exceptional service and authentic experiences sets us apart, and we strive to make every visit to Turkmenistan an unforgettable one.

Our mission is to provide travelers with the opportunity to explore Turkmenistan's hidden gems, from its stunning natural wonders to its vibrant cultural heritage. Whether you're interested in historical sites, natural landscapes, or cultural experiences, our diverse range of tours and packages are designed to cater to all types of travelers.

Thank you for choosing Gatnashyk Travel. We look forward to welcoming you to Turkmenistan and helping you create memories that will last a lifetime.
