Ancient Merv

Ancient Merv, one of the most significant archaeological sites in Turkmenistan, offers a fascinating glimpse into the grandeur of the Silk Road era. Once a thriving metropolis, Merv was a key trading hub and cultural center, renowned for its impressive architecture and historical significance.

About Merv

Ancient Merv, located near the modern city of Mary, was an influential city in the ancient world, with its history spanning from the Achaemenid period to the Islamic Golden Age. Visiting Tips

When visiting Ancient Merv, plan to spend a full day exploring the site to fully appreciate its vastness and historical depth. Wear comfortable walking shoes, bring water, and be prepared for the desert climate. It is also advisable to hire a local guide to gain a deeper understanding of the site's history and significance.
