The Introduction

Gatnashyk Travel
Unlock the Secrets of Turkmenistan with the Experts

Welcome to Gatnashyk Travel, your gateway to the mesmerizing landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture of Turkmenistan. Nestled at the crossroads of ancient civilizations, Turkmenistan offers a journey through time, where the echoes of the Silk Road resonate in the golden dunes of the Karakum Desert, and the architectural wonders of Ashgabat shine with modern splendor.

Whether you're drawn to the enigmatic ruins of Merv, the breathtaking beauty of the Yangykala Canyon, or the timeless traditions of Turkmen hospitality, our expertly crafted tours will immerse you in the heart of this extraordinary land. From serene oases and ancient cities to the warmth of local communities, every moment in Turkmenistan is an opportunity to explore the unfamiliar and create unforgettable memories.

Explore Turkmenistan

Ancient Wonders

Discover the ancient civilizations of Turkmenistan, home to UNESCO World Heritage Sites like Kunya-Urgench, Nisa, and Merv. These historic landmarks offer a glimpse into the grandeur of ancient empires that once thrived along the Silk Road.

  • Learn More

Natural Landscapes

Explore Turkmenistan's breathtaking natural beauty, from the vast Karakum Desert to the colorful Yangykala Canyon and the adventurous Koytendag Mountains. These landscapes offer you scenery and opportunities for adventure in untouched nature.

  • Learn More

Modern Marvels

Experience the futuristic charm of Ashgabat with its marble-clad architecture and modern monuments like the Monument of Neutrality. Stroll through Independence Park, marvel a the state of the art Turkmenbashi Airport, and enjoy the serene beauty of the city's fountains, all reflecting Turkmenistan's vision for modern development and national pride.

  • Learn More

Cultural Experiences

Immerse yourself in Turkmenistan's rich cultural heritage by visiting the Turkmen Carpet Museum, witnessing the grace of Ahal-Teke horses, and exploring traditional bazaars. These experiences showcase the country's deep-rooted traditions and vibrant local culture.
