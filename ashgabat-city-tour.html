Tour Details

Ashgabat city tour

Ashgabat

Discover the architectural marvels and rich history of Ashgabat, Turkmenistan's vibrant capital. Our guided city tour offers a unique glimpse into the grandeur of this city, renowned for its white marble buildings, stunning monuments, and lush green spaces.

Itinerary

Day 1

  • Meeting at Ashgabat International Airport.
  • Transfer and check-in at the hotel.
  • Visit to shopping and entertainment centers "Berkarar".
  • Excursion to the National Museum of Turkmen Carpets.

Day 2

  • Excursion to Old Nisa - included in the UNESCO World Heritage List, one of the first capitals of the Parthian Empire.
  • Visit to the private horse farm Gokdepe.
  • Visit to the local market "Gulistan" (Russian bazaar).

Day 3

  • Sightseeing tour of Ashgabat: Independence Monument, Arch of Neutrality, Constitution Monument, Alem Cultural and Entertainment Center, Magtymguly Fragi Park, Memorial Complex, Wedding Palace.
  • Visit to the shopping and entertainment center "Arkach".

Day 4

  • Free time.
  • Transfer to the airport for flight to Ashgabat.
